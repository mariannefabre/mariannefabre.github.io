About me

Born and raised in Paris, evolving between french and south korean cultures, I quickly developped curiosity to other cultures. After getting my Bachelor's in engineering, I went to Montreal for my first year of Master's, where I specialized in software engineering. Being back in Paris, I worked 6 months as an intern at VMware, before graduating in 2018. At that point, despite being drawn by new technologies and software development I decided to keep as many doors open as possible and moved to Hamburg to work as an engineer consultant for Airbus. Living abroad and working in an international context is enriching and working as a consultant is a good way to try out different things. After two years I finally took/made the step to focus on what I always wanted to do: create products: I quited my job and took a self-learning course to become a Front-End Engineer. Today I am still learning and look forward to my journey as a Front-End Engineer.
