About me

❝ I was born and raised in Paris, influenced by both my french and south korean background. After obtaining my Bachelor's degree in engineering, I spent part of my Master's in Montréal, where I specialized in software engineering. Before graduating in 2018, I completed a six month internship at VMware in Paris. Despite being drawn to new technologies and software development, I accepted an opportunity to work in Hamburg as an engineering consultant for Airbus. Living abroad and working in an international context is enriching, and working as a consultant offers a diverse and varied range of work. After two years, I decided to focus on realizing my ambition to design and develop IT products. I therefore left my job to pursue a front-end engineering course. Today I continue to enjoy learning about software development and working towards a career as a Front-End Engineer. ❞
